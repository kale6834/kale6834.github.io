Framing Letter

The works that I have written are the forum analysis, the literature review, and the white paper. The forum analysis and literature review works were targeted towards an audience with a more scientific background. Even though I tried my best to explain terms and provide background information, someone with a scientific background would have had an easier time understanding these papers than someone without that knowledge.

The forum analysis has an integration between video games and psychology. The popular article is written for an audience interested in the science behind gamification, a subsection of psychology, and the scientific article is a scientific study about gamification and is written for an audience that is studying the subject or has more knowledge on the subject. The literature review was definitely targeted towards an audience with prior knowledge of computer science and maybe some medical knowledge.

I believe that I wrote well for the audiences with prior background knowledge. It is hard to tell if I wrote well for audiences without prior knowledge because I at least have some understanding of the subjects I wrote about. I tried my best to break down and explain information for those that might not understand it to begin with.

Ways that I incorporated ideas and knowledge in a coherent and meaningful manner is mostly in the introduction of my papers. The white paper is the best example because I had a background information section that described the different types of healthcare and current events before suggesting that universal healthcare is a solution that could help the uninsured and/or underinsured Americans. The literature review, in which I wrote about the use of machine learning in human medicine, has small sections of each subject explaining them in detail.

I believe that my best document is the literature review because I have written that type of work before, so it came naturally to me. I thought it was written more smoothly and flowed better than the other works I did. Because I am a very scientific person, I enjoyed writing this more than the others. It was easier for me to find facts and transfer them into a literature review. There was not much creative writing to it.

I decided to revise the forum analysis. I added in more to the introduction, so it doesn’t dive right into the articles that were used. I did my best to add more to the comparisons page. The summary for the scholarly articles wasn’t shortened as advised, but I removed some of the bullet points and made it more paragraph-like.
